1

Complete the Assessment

Access the Localization Competencies Self-Evaluation Tool. Work through all sections honestly and thoughtfully. When you finish, save your results as a PDF.

2

Analyze the Tool

As you use the assessment tool, observe its functionality and components. Write 1-3 sentences responding to these questions:

  • What components does the tool include?
  • How would you approach localizing this application?
3

Reflect on Your Results

Review your competency scores and write 1-3 sentences responding to:

  • Why did you receive the scores you did? Do you agree with them?
  • Did any results surprise you?
4

Consider Your Motivations

Write 1-3 sentences responding to:

  • Why are you interested in website localization?
5

Save Your Work

If you're completing this assignment as part of a class, submit your PDF and written responses in the format requested by your instructor. If you're working independently, save all materials in an organized folder so you can reflect on your growth as you complete the course.
