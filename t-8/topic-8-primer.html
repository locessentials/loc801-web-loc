TOPIC 8

CMS & WordPress

Most websites you will localize in the real world are not built from scratch in a code editor. They run on a Content Management System. This topic introduces you to how CMS platforms work, what localization looks like inside the most widely used one, and how to scope and quote a real CMS project.

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand what a Content Management System is and why it matters for localization work
  • Recognize what localization involves inside WordPress, including themes, plugins, language files, and the wp-admin interface
  • Identify the different places where localizable content can live in a CMS-hosted site
  • Research a CMS platform and assess its implications for a localization project
  • Scope a small website, request real vendor quotes, and evaluate them using your localization knowledge
